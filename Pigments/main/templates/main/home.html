About Our Project

Welcome to our project, inspired by the extensive collection of items donated to the Kerr Hamilton Institute by Roberson and Co. and other contributors. This collection serves as a comprehensive archive that vividly illustrates the manufacturing and painting industry of 19th-century England. Our primary goal is to bring various elements of this archive to light, with a special focus on pigments. The collection includes over 300 vials filled with a diverse array of pigments and materials. The purpose of this website is to showcase these elements, making them freely and easily accessible for exploration. We have created a digital edition of a manuscript where Charles Roberson and others documented their recipes for creating and composing pigments, as well as a database of many materials from the archive. This initiative is designed to be expandable, paving the way for future studies and discoveries in this fascinating field.
